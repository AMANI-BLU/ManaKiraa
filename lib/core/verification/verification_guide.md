# Property Verification Process

To ensure trust and safety in the Mana Kiraa marketplace, we verify properties posted by landlords.

## Steps for Landlords
1. **Navigate to "My Properties"**: Found under the Settings tab.
2. **Click "Verify"**: On any property that is not yet verified.
3. **Upload Documents**:
    - **Valid ID Card**: Government-issued ID.
    - **Ownership Proof**: Title deed or legal rental agreement.
    - **Utility Bill**: Recent bill showing the property address.
4. **Wait for Review**: Our administrative team will review your documents manually.

## Verification Status
- **Pending**: Icon shows that documents are being reviewed.
- **Verified**: A blue badge appears on your property listing, increasing visibility and trust.
- **Rejected**: You will receive a notification if the documentation is insufficient.

---
*Note: Verified properties get 3x more views on average.*
